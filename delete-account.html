Delete your Kotiki account & data

Kotiki is a cozy pixel-art cat game by Ivan Zaika. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time.

From inside the app

  1. Open Settings (the gear icon).
  2. In the Cloud Save section, tap Delete account & data.
  3. Confirm. Your account and all progress are permanently deleted — both in the cloud and on this device — and the app returns to the start screen.

By email

If you can no longer open the app, email vanzai.studio@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account (Apple or Google), or include your in-app Account ID (shown in Settings → Cloud Save). We will delete your account and data within 30 days.

What is deleted

Your Firebase sign-in account, your cloud save (cats, room, progress), and the local game data on your device. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone, and we do not keep a copy. Purchase records are held by the App Store / Google Play, not by us, and are not affected by this request.

Contact: vanzai.studio@gmail.com

Dein Firebase-Anmeldekonto, dein Cloud-Speicher (Katzen, Zimmer, Fortschritt) und die lokalen Spieldaten auf deinem Gerät. Die Löschung ist dauerhaft und kann nicht rückgängig gemacht werden; wir behalten keine Kopie.
